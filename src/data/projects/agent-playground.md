A scratchpad for agent and automation experiments. The idea is to have a space where I can test things without the pressure of shipping a product.

## What it is

Most automation experiments start as a one-off script or a quick test in a REPL. This playground gives those experiments a consistent place to live — somewhere I can revisit, extend, or throw away without guilt.

## What I explore here

- Agent flows with tool calling
- Prompt patterns that actually work in practice
- Small automations that remove annoying manual steps
- Chaining outputs from one tool into inputs for another

## Why keep it private (for now)

Most of it is rough by design. The value is in the learning, not the output. Some of it might turn into a proper project eventually.
