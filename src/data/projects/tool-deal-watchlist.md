A simple personal tracker for software deals and tool notes. Built because I kept losing track of lifetime deals I had bought and forgetting why I bought them.

## The problem

There are always deals floating around — AppSumo, PitchGround, various indie launches. Some are genuinely useful. Most are not. And after buying something, it is easy to forget what it was even for.

## What it does

- Stores tools I own or am watching
- Lets me add a short note on why I added it
- Reminds me whether something is actually in my workflow or just sitting unused

## Status

Works for personal use. Not polished enough to share publicly, but it solves the problem it was built for.
